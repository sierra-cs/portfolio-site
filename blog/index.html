Howdy, I'm Sierra Stephenson - the creative mind behind SierraCS Studio, where I blend web design, front-end development, and a whole lot of Texas pride into every project I touch.

I'm an Black American designer and developer born and raised in Henderson, Texas. My love for computers started young, but it really clicked during my senior year of high school when I got the opportunity to work with my school district's tech department. Being hands-on with troubleshooting, problem-solving, and seeing how technology could transform everyday operations? That sealed the deal. I knew this was my path.

I recently graduated with my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from The University of Phoenix, where I built a strong foundation in technical skills and creative problem-solving. Now, I'm focused on crafting beautiful, functional websites for small businesses, entrepreneurs, and growing brands - online spaces that are as strategic as they are stylish.
